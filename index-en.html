It's being said "As many languages you know, as many times you are a human being".

In our case it's more like as many countries we visit, as many times we are human beings. Every nation is different, every culture is unique in it's own way and people are unlike as well. Meeting other people helps us to see life from a different perspective. Travelling plays a big role in our lives. Not only we discover new places, we also reinvent ourselves. After all, when man travels, he has lots of time to think. Moreover, we can get into situations with which we have to deal by ourselves and improvise a little while travelling. We learn new things. Exactly these moments and much more we would like to share with you. We also want to show you that even the laziest students like us can travel the world. Talk about our experiences, help you with to discover the most beautiful, breathtaking places, especially those which aren't that popular, but very special. We'd like to warn you, tell you what to be aware of and when to be more careful, in order not to make the same mistakes as we did. We're going to add other countries we visited one by one. There's lots to be looking forward to!
